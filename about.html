About This Blog

Welcome to My Simple Blog! This site was created as a learning project to understand the fundamentals of web development. My goal is to explore HTML, CSS, and eventually, static site generators.

On this blog, I'll be documenting my journey, sharing what I've learned, and providing tips and tricks for fellow beginners. Expect to find posts on basic coding practices, styling techniques, and how to build a simple, responsive website from scratch.

About Me

I'm a beginner developer with a passion for creating and learning. I believe that the best way to learn is by doing, which is why I built this blog. When I'm not coding, you can find me reading about new technologies or enjoying a cup of coffee.

This project is the first step in what I hope will be a long and exciting career in tech. Thanks for stopping by!
